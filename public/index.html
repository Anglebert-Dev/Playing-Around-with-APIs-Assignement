    About Boredom Buster

    Boredom Buster helps you discover exciting activities and meaningful advice when you're feeling stuck. Whether you're looking for educational pursuits, social activities, or creative projects, we've got you covered.

    Our app combines the power of the Bored API for activity suggestions and the Advice Slip API for wisdom and guidance.
